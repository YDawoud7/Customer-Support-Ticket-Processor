# Company Policies

## Privacy Policy Summary
- We collect only the data necessary to provide our services.
- Customer data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3).
- We do not sell customer data to third parties.
- Customers can request data export or deletion at any time via Account Settings > Privacy > Data Request.
- Data is stored in US-based data centers (AWS us-east-1 and us-west-2).
- Enterprise customers may request data residency in EU (Frankfurt) or APAC (Sydney).
- Full privacy policy: acmecorp.com/privacy

## Terms of Service Summary
- Users must be 16 years or older to create an account.
- Accounts are for individual use unless on a team plan.
- We reserve the right to suspend accounts that violate our acceptable use policy.
- Content ownership: customers retain full ownership of their content. Acme Corp has a limited license to host and display content only for service delivery.
- Service availability target: 99.9% uptime (Enterprise SLA: 99.95%).
- Full terms: acmecorp.com/terms

## Acceptable Use Policy
Prohibited activities include:
- Using the platform to store or distribute malware.
- Automated scraping or bulk data extraction.
- Sharing accounts or credentials with unauthorized users.
- Using the service for illegal activities.
- Storing regulated data (HIPAA, PCI) without an Enterprise plan with compliance add-on.

Violations result in a warning for first offense, temporary suspension for second offense, and permanent termination for third offense.

## Accessibility Commitment
- Acme Workspace meets WCAG 2.1 AA standards.
- Screen reader compatible (tested with NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver).
- Keyboard navigation supported throughout the application.
- High contrast mode available in Settings > Accessibility.
- Report accessibility issues: accessibility@acmecorp.com
