# Invoice Guide

## Invoice Format
Every invoice includes the following fields:
- **Invoice Number**: Unique identifier in the format INV-YYYYMMDD-XXXX.
- **Order Number**: Links to the original order (format: ORD-XXXXX).
- **Date Issued**: The date the invoice was generated.
- **Due Date**: Payment due date (typically 30 days from issue date).
- **Line Items**: Description, quantity, unit price, and total for each item.
- **Subtotal**: Sum of all line items before tax.
- **Tax**: Applicable sales tax based on billing address.
- **Total**: Final amount due including tax.

## Accessing Invoices
Customers can access their invoices through:
1. **Account Dashboard**: Navigate to Billing > Invoices to view and download all invoices.
2. **Email**: Invoices are automatically emailed when generated.
3. **Support Request**: Customers can request invoice copies by contacting support with their order number.

## Common Invoice Issues
### Missing Invoice
If an invoice is not visible in the dashboard:
- Check the email associated with the account for the emailed copy.
- Verify the order was completed (pending orders do not generate invoices).
- Contact support with the order number to request a re-send.

### Incorrect Amount
If the invoice amount does not match expectations:
- Compare with the order confirmation email for discrepancies.
- Check if a discount code was applied correctly.
- Verify tax calculations based on your billing address.
- Contact billing support for adjustment if an error is confirmed.

## Payment Methods
Accepted payment methods include:
- Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- ACH bank transfers (for invoices over $500)
- Wire transfers (for enterprise accounts)
- PayPal (for personal accounts only)
