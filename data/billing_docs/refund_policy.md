# Refund Policy

## Standard Refund Window
All purchases are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of the original transaction date. After 30 days, refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

## Refund Process
1. Customer submits a refund request with order number and reason.
2. Billing team verifies the purchase and checks eligibility.
3. If approved, the refund is processed within 5-7 business days.
4. The refund is credited to the original payment method.

## Duplicate Charges
If a customer was charged more than once for the same order:
- The duplicate charge is always eligible for an immediate refund.
- No 30-day window applies to duplicate charge corrections.
- Processing time for duplicate charge refunds is 3-5 business days.
- Customer should provide the order number and both transaction IDs if available.

## Partial Refunds
- Subscription services are refunded on a pro-rated basis for the unused portion of the billing period.
- Bundle purchases may be partially refunded if only some items are returned.
- Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to a company error.

## Non-Refundable Items
- Digital downloads that have been accessed or downloaded.
- Gift cards and promotional credits.
- Services that have been fully rendered.
- Items marked as "final sale" at the time of purchase.

## Escalation
If a refund request is denied and the customer disagrees, they may request escalation to a billing supervisor. Escalation reviews are completed within 2 business days.
