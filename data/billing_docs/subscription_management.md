# Subscription Management

## Plans Available
| Plan | Monthly Price | Annual Price | Features |
|------|--------------|-------------|----------|
| Starter | $9.99/mo | $99/yr | Basic access, 5 projects, email support |
| Professional | $29.99/mo | $299/yr | Full access, unlimited projects, priority support |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | Custom integrations, dedicated account manager, SLA |

## Upgrading a Plan
- Upgrades take effect immediately.
- The billing difference is pro-rated for the remaining billing period.
- No additional action needed; features unlock automatically upon upgrade.

## Downgrading a Plan
- Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period.
- No refund is issued for the current period; the customer retains access to higher-tier features until the period ends.
- Data exceeding the lower plan's limits is preserved for 30 days, then archived.

## Cancellation
- Customers can cancel at any time from Account Settings > Subscription > Cancel.
- Access continues until the end of the current billing period.
- After cancellation, the account switches to a free tier with limited access.
- Reactivation within 30 days restores all previous data and settings.
- After 30 days, data is permanently deleted.

## Billing Cycle
- Monthly subscriptions are billed on the same day each month (based on the original sign-up date).
- Annual subscriptions are billed once per year on the anniversary date.
- Failed payments trigger a 7-day grace period with three retry attempts before suspension.
- Customers receive email notifications at each retry and upon suspension.

## Disputes
If a customer disputes a subscription charge:
- Verify the subscription status and billing history in the admin panel.
- Check if the customer attempted to cancel before the charge date.
- Issue a refund if the charge occurred after a valid cancellation request.
